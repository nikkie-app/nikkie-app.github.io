Frequently Asked Questions

Q. My artwork isn't being generated

Please check your network connection. Also note that there is a limit of one artwork per day, so if you've already generated today's artwork, please try again tomorrow.

Q. How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or through the Google Play Store on Android.

Q. How do I delete my account?

Go to the "Settings" screen within the app and select "Delete Account." Once deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed.

Q. Can I freely use the generated artwork?

Yes, you are free to use the generated artwork for personal purposes. Feel free to share it on social media or use it as your phone wallpaper.
